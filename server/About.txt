Thia is Iris Flower Classification Model (updating...)
Flowers Length and Width :
Iris Setosa : 
        The sepal length of Iris setosa is typically not greater than 6.0 cm 
        The sepal width is not less than 2.3 cm.
        The petal length of Iris setosa is not greater than 2 cm 
        The petal width is not greater than 0.6 cm.
Iris virginica : 
        The sepal length for Iris virginica typically ranges from 4.9 to 7.0 cm
        The sepal width ranges from 2.0 to 3.0 cm.
        The petal length ranges from 4.0 to 6.9 cm
        The petal width ranges from 1.4 to 2.5 cm.
Iris versicolor:
        The sepal length typically ranges from 4.9 to 7.0 cm.
        The sepal width ranges from 2.0 to 3.4 cm.
        The petal length is usually between 3.0 and 5.1 cm.
        The petal width is generally between 1.0 and 1.8 cm.
